What is DormBites

DormBites is an innovative web application designed for students, especially anyone living in dormitories or small apartments. The platform offers a comprehensive collection of recipes, cooking methods, and creative ways to utilize leftover food, helping users make the most out of their limited kitchen space and resources.


In addition to providing easy-to-follow recipes, DormBites also features a user-friendly interface with step-by-step cooking guides, video tutorials, and a smart ingredient search tool that suggests meals based on what users already have. The platform encourages sustainable cooking habits by offering tips on food storage, meal prepping, and reducing waste, making it a valuable resource for those looking to save time, money, and effort while maintaining a healthy and eco-friendly lifestyle.

Our Objectives

Reduce Food Waste: Many people often throw away leftovers without realizing they can be repurposed into new, tasty meals. By suggesting creative ways to use what’s left, food waste can be significantly reduced, promoting a more sustainable lifestyle.

Promote Resourceful Way of Living: By utilizing available ingredients and minimizing unnecessary purchases, users can adopt a more resourceful lifestyle. This encourages smarter food consumption, helping individuals save money and reduce waste.

Innovate Healthy Food Recipes: Finding healthy meals that fit into a busy lifestyle is challenging. Offering innovative recipes using existing ingredients ensures that eating healthy remains convenient, affordable, and enjoyable.

Suggest Budget-friendly eateries: Eating out doesn’t have to be expensive. Affordable dining options that meet users’ budgets and preferences are easily accessible, ensuring users can enjoy good meals without overspending.
